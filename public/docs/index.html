Turn a workout plan into a fast, local-first tracker.

Import a plan you already have — a document, spreadsheet, or PDF — and simpleSet turns it into a day-by-day tracker you can log sets against, watch your progress on, and take with you. No account. No server. Your data stays on your device.

Open simpleSet →

What it does

  • Imports a plan from a Word doc, spreadsheet, PDF, plain text, or a file in your Google Drive
  • Tracks sets, reps, weight, and time against your plan, day by day
  • Detects personal records automatically as you log
  • Shows streaks, consistency, and progress over time
  • Works offline and installs to your home screen like a native app

How your data is handled

simpleSet stores everything locally in your browser — there's no account, no sign-in, and no server that your workout data passes through. See the privacy policy for details, including how the optional Google Drive import works.
